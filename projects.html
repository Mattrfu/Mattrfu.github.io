PROJECTS

Short Story Recommender

I created a short story recommender that used unsupervised clustering based on user comments and like patterns to generate story recommendations. The user comments and like patterns were scraped.

AP CS A and AP CS Principles Auto Grader

We made an auto-grader using the Mimir platform to allow students to submit PSET solutions for grading. Students in AP CS A and Principles could use the platform for immediate feedback and grades.

Simple Attendance Tracker

We created a simple attendance tracker for a large high school robotics team that was focused on tracking how many hours someone was working on their project. With functionality that included check-ins, check-outs, hour confirmations, deletes, history, and a roster, the project was meant to give the team the tools it needed to really find out how many hours went into every project.
